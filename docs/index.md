# Seeton Video Post

Seeton Video Post is a personal automation project that discovers safe trending topics, drafts a short narration, generates audio with a local Aivis server, composes a vertical video with Remotion, and uploads the result to the creator's own TikTok account through the official TikTok Content Posting API.

This site hosts the legal documents required by the TikTok for Developers registration process.
